About

I am a Senior Algorithm Engineer at Ant Group, working on wearable health intelligence. My current focus is on using LLMs to support health understanding and interpretation from long-term wearable data. Previously, I worked at Alibaba DAMO Academy (DAMO-DI-M Lab) on foundation models for weather forecasting and LLM-based time-series analysis.
